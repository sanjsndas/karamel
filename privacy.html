    Our Commitment to Privacy

    At Karamel Bread Whisper, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure it's kept secure.

    1. Information We Collect

    We may collect the following types of information:

    • Personal Identification: Name, email address, and any other details you provide through our contact forms.
    • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website, such as pages visited and time spent.
    • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience (see our Cookies Policy).

    2. How We Use Your Information

    The information we collect helps us to:

    • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
    • Improve our website, content, and user experience.
    • Send you updates, newsletters, or other information you've requested (you can opt out at any time).

    3. Data Security

    We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored on secure servers and is only accessible by a limited number of authorized personnel.

    4. Sharing Your Information

    We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification regarding visitors and users with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers for the purposes outlined above.

    5. Your Consent

    By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our website.

    6. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

    We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the updated date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to frequently check this page for any changes to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information we collect.

    7. Contacting Us

    If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us using the information below:
